HOW WE MAKE DIFFERENCE

Our patient department(OPD)

Our OPD is a facility that is responsible for providing prevention, cure, and alleviation of diseases and pain. Our OPD clinic is a fully functional facility which is equipped to handle critical emergencies.

Critical Medical Care

There are so many animals day in & day out that are subjected to cruelty, road rage and medical concerns. Our NGO works towards helping such animals either in our OPD or by admitting them into our facility.

Feeding good food

Stray animals are mostly deprived of good food. We aim at providing healthy and nutritious food to them.
